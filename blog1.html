Why RSS Royal Biryani is the Best Biryani in Vizag

The journey to creating the best biryani in Vizag begins with understanding the science and art behind dum cooking. Dum cooking is an ancient Indian culinary technique that involves slow-cooking ingredients in a sealed vessel, allowing flavors to intensify and infuse into every grain of basmati rice. This traditional method has been perfected at RSS Royal Biryani, making our biryani preparation process a masterclass in patience and precision.

Our chicken dum biryani represents the pinnacle of non-veg restaurant offerings in Visakhapatnam. We start with premium chicken sourced fresh daily, marinated in traditional spices including yogurt, ginger, garlic, and our secret blend of aromatics. The chicken is then layered with partially cooked fragrant Afghan basmati rice and sealed in heavy-bottomed vessels. Over 45-50 minutes, the biryani cooks in its own steam, creating an aromatic masterpiece that defines what authentic dum biryani should taste like.
